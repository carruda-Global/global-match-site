Can an AI Receptionist Provide Directions to My Business?

Location and navigation features, and what to actually configure.

This is a smaller but genuinely useful piece of what an AI receptionist can handle — especially for businesses in office parks, strip malls, or locations that are easy to drive past.

What it can realistically provide

  • Your full address and nearby cross streets or landmarks
  • Parking instructions — which lot, street parking, validation policies
  • Which entrance to use, especially for multi-suite buildings
  • Notable "you'll know you're close when..." context that a caller actually finds useful

What it isn't

It's not a turn-by-turn GPS system — most callers already have a map app doing that job, and duplicating it adds little value. The useful part is the context a map app doesn't know: which door to actually use, that the visible sign is for a different business on the same block, or that parking requires a code.

Configuration tip: when setting this up, think like a first-time visitor who's never been to your location — what would confuse them that a generic address wouldn't fix? That's the information worth adding.
