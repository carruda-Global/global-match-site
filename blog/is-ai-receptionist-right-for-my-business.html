How Do I Know If an AI Receptionist Is Right for My Business?

Decision-making criteria, not a sales pitch.

Rather than a generic "everyone should get one," here's a practical way to actually check whether this fits your specific situation.

Signs it's a strong fit

  • You regularly miss calls during business hours because staff is busy elsewhere
  • Calls after hours or on weekends currently go to voicemail nobody checks promptly
  • A large share of your calls are repeatable — hours, pricing, booking, availability
  • Staff time is being eaten up by repetitive phone questions instead of higher-value work
  • You're considering hiring purely to keep up with call volume, not for other reasons

Signs it's a weaker fit right now

  • Very low call volume overall — the absolute time savings will be small
  • Nearly every call is a unique, complex conversation that genuinely needs a person's judgment
  • You already have reliable, well-staffed phone coverage with no missed-call problem
