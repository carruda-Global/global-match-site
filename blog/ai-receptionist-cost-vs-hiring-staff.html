The costs staffing quotes usually leave out

  • Payroll tax and benefits — typically adds 15-25% on top of base wages
  • Training time — weeks before a new hire handles calls confidently
  • Sick days, PTO, and turnover — coverage gaps you have to plan around or absorb
  • The gap hours — nobody's staffing the phone at 7pm or on Sunday unless you pay extra for it
What this isn't: a claim that AI replaces a skilled front-desk employee's judgment. It's a much cheaper way to guarantee the phone gets answered — see whether you still need a human receptionist for how the two actually complement each other.
