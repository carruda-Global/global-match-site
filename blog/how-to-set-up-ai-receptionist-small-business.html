How Do I Set Up an AI Receptionist for My Small Business?

The real implementation process, not the sales-page version.

Setup is usually the point where owners either commit or bail, because a complicated, sales-call-gated onboarding process is a bad sign about the product itself. Here's what a real setup actually involves.

The three things setup actually requires

  1. Connect your number — either forward your existing business line or get a new one, depending on the vendor
  2. Feed it your business information — hours, pricing, services, common questions, and how you want it to talk about your business
  3. Set your escalation rules — what happens when it can't resolve a call: transfer, message, or callback

That's the entire process for a self-service platform. AION does this in under 20 minutes with no developer and no mandatory sales call — you can go from signup to a live, answering phone number in one sitting.
