Will an AI Receptionist Work with My Current Phone System?

Compatibility and integration concerns, answered directly.

This is a practical, deployment-level question, and the good news is that most AI receptionist setups are simpler than owners expect — no new hardware, no ripped-out phone system.

How the connection typically works

  • Call forwarding — the most common setup: your existing number forwards to the AI system, so nothing changes from a customer's perspective
  • New number — alternatively, some businesses get a new dedicated number for the AI receptionist and use it going forward
  • Works across systems — this generally doesn't depend on whether you're on a landline, a VoIP provider, or a mobile number, since forwarding operates at the carrier level

What to confirm before setup

If you're on a complex existing setup — a multi-line PBX, a call center system, or an enterprise phone platform — confirm directly with your vendor how forwarding or routing will actually work in your specific case. For a typical small business on a standard landline, VoIP line, or cell number, this is close to plug-and-play.
