FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a developer to set this up?

No. Setup is self-service and takes under 20 minutes — connect your number, and you're live.

What happens if I go over 300 minutes?

Extra minutes are billed at $0.15/min — no plan upgrade required, no service interruption.

Can I try it before I buy?

Yes — call the live demo number above and experience the exact call flow your customers would get, no signup needed.

Does it work with my existing business number?

Yes — forward your existing line to your AI receptionist, or use a new dedicated number, your choice.

How is this different from a regular answering service?

A human answering service charges per call or per minute with a live agent reading a script. AION is a flat $89/mo AI voice agent that answers instantly, every time, with no queue and no per-call fee. See the full AI receptionist vs. answering service comparison.

Will my customers know they're talking to an AI?

Yes — every call opens with a clear, spoken disclosure that it's an AI assistant, and the conversation is recorded for quality and lead-capture purposes. We don't try to pass it off as a human.

What happens to calls the AI can't handle?

The agent transfers to your business line, takes a detailed message, or offers a callback — whichever you configure. You're notified so nothing falls through the cracks.

Can I customize what the AI says about my business?

Yes — every account has its own knowledge base (hours, services, pricing, FAQs) so the agent answers with your actual business details, not a generic script.

What if I need more than 300 minutes a month?

Growth plan customers get a second line and higher included minutes for multi-location or high-call-volume businesses — see plans for details.
