Why a missed call is worse than it looks

A caller who reaches voicemail rarely leaves a message — most simply call the next business on the list. There's no error message, no bounced email, nothing that shows up in your inbox. The only signal is a caller ID you never called back, and by the time anyone notices the pattern, months of it have already happened.

Three ways an AI receptionist stops the leak

1. Every call gets answered. No voicemail, no ringing out — a caller reaches a real greeting 24/7, including after hours and on weekends.
2. Missed calls get an instant text-back. If a call can't be handled live, the caller gets a text immediately with a way to reach you — most people never leave a voicemail, but almost everyone reads a text.
3. Every lead is logged, not lost. Name, number, and reason for calling — captured automatically, so follow-up isn't dependent on someone remembering to check voicemail.
